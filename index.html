ABOUT ME

A tech enthusiast turned Web Developer
from the San Francisco Bay Area, CA.

As I was working in quality control to oversee the auto body assembly process for Tesla M3, I realized in such tech-driven era, a user friendly web is crucial because it is the window to most any databases, processes and communications among clients and suppliers. To better equip myself with skills necessary to solve database related complex problems, I started teaching myself coding and eventually went through an accelerated web development program. There, my desire to create and problem-solve reignited which led me to becoming a competent programmer.

I love programming because it is mentally stimulating in various ways. The satisfaction that comes from seeing a desired result after a long mental fight is indescribable. Also I'm a soccer enthusiast and enjoy watching and playing whenever I get a chance. I also like to cook, and spending time with my ever hungry cats.
